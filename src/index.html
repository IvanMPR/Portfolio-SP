About me

Hello and welcome, I'm Ivan Miceta from Belgrade, Serbia, and I'm self taught web developer. On this page you can get familiar with my developer skills, resources I used while learning and detailed biography.

In the spring of 2020, I decided to invest time end effort to start learning web development. That road was hard, sometimes painful, and just one simple thing kept me moving forward.

I fell in love with coding.

The joy and satisfaction felt when I solved 'FizzBuzz' challenge, when my first project appeared online on Github, or CodeWars problem solved was overwhelming and contagious.

It superseded, by far, all frustrations, disappointments, doubts and obstacles that are present and inevitable when trying to learn such a complex and vast topic such as a programming language.

I sincerely hope that after reviewing this portfolio, you will find my time well spent and my self-proclaimed 'title' of web developer is not undeserved.
